## Diagnostic emphasis

- sort the data before applying median conditions and count repetitions before fixing a mode
- write mean, median, and mode conditions separately before combining them
- when a score is added, update the total and count independently
- convert a range condition into constraints on the smallest and largest values
- distinguish one-group interpretation from two-group comparison in stem-and-leaf plots
- reconstruct class intervals before counting classes or missing frequencies
- calculate percentages as `selected frequency / total frequency x 100`
- restore torn histograms from total-frequency, ratio, percentage, and rectangle-area conditions
- read a frequency polygon at class marks and include zero-frequency endpoints correctly
- identify the denominator of relative frequency as total frequency
- use the sum of relative frequencies as 1 when a table or graph is torn
- convert a recovered relative frequency back to an actual frequency only after identifying the total
